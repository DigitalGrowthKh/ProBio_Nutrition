How do gut health and mental health connect?

The gut and brain are connected through the gut-brain axis. A balanced microbiome can positively influence neurotransmitter production, impacting mood, clarity, and emotional stability.

What are the signs of poor gut health and how can a holistic coach help?

Common signs of an imbalanced microbiome include bloating, fatigue, food intolerances, and even mood swings or anxiety. As a holistic wellness coach, Igor Katchkin uses a tailored approach combining targeted probiotic nutrition with lifestyle coaching to restore your gut flora and overall vitality.

How does the gut-brain axis affect anxiety and mental wellness?

Your gut and brain communicate constantly through the gut-brain axis. An unhealthy gut can disrupt the production of key neurotransmitters like serotonin, leading to brain fog, stress, and anxiety. Healing your digestive system with proper probiotics and holistic psychological support can significantly improve your emotional stability.
